About

I’m Amawin, a Sri Lankan Ceylon Producer and Rapper, representing my culture through music and storytelling. From Raigampura streets to the studio, I’ve built my craft step by step — producing beats, writing lyrics, and delivering raw, real bars that connect with my audience.

Over the years, I’ve collaborated with talented artists, released tracks under my label KrownBase Entertainment, and created music videos that reflect the struggles, dreams, and energy of the streets. My goal is to inspire, entertain, and push the boundaries of what it means to be an independent artist.

Music isn’t just my passion; it’s my mission — a way to share stories, unite listeners, and leave a legacy that lasts.
